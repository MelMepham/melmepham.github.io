Motivation

Living by your beliefs.

Monday the 11th of December

I have been searching for something the really fulfils me most of my life, I knew that thing was with a high level of creativity, I also knew that money was not an aspiration and I wanted to have a positive effect on my surroundings. it has all been very confusing trying to find something that ticks all of these boxes, I have been told constantly through my life I have to have a stable job, and how passion doesn’t mix with work. I don’t agree, and have struggled through my life trying to find that balance, I am yet to find it, but I know it is there. This is why I like the idea of code, it merges those things together for me.

I have spent a lot of time thinking about what I stand for, and what my core values are.. I have used a Holistic Management tool to really look at who I am, what I want to achieve, and how best to live by my morals to get there.

I exist to live authentically. To learn and grow the best I can in order to have a positive impact on my community and environment


  • I actively work to keep a high level of mental and physical health
  • Life {living beings} always come before the material world
  • I engage with authenticity, compassion without judgement
  • I act with integrity to leave the world habitable for life after I am gone
  • I engage in meaningful professional and creative work

I try to reflect back on these when making decisions, and if they meet these requirements then I go ahead, if not I rethink. It is a great way to check in with myself and make sure I am living by what I believe.
